# Coffee-Sales-Analysis-Excel
Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard Project

☕Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard.

📌Project Overview:

This project is an end-to-end data analysis of a coffee shop's sales performance. Using Microsoft Excel, raw sales data was cleaned, processed, and transformed into an interactive and dynamic dashboard. The goal of this project is to provide actionable insights into customer purchasing behavior, peak sales periods, and product popularity to help optimize business operations, inventory, and staffing.

🛠️Tools & Technologies Used:

MS Excel: Primary tool for data manipulation and visualization.

Power Query Editor: Used for data cleaning, transformation, and shaping.

Data Modeling: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) and KPI building for advanced metric calculations.

Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts: For aggregating data and creating the core visual components.

Interactive Elements: Slicers and Timelines allowing users to filter data dynamically by Day, Time, Payment Type, and Month.

📊Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

Total Sales: $115,431.58

Total Transactions: 3,636

Average Order Value (AOV): $31.75

📈Dashboard Features & Visualizations:

The interactive dashboard features a custom aesthetic design and includes the following analytical views:

Sales Trend Analysis: Line charts displaying Total Sales, Total Transactions, and Average Order Value over the months.

Time & Day Breakdown: Pie charts illustrating sales distribution by Time of Day (Morning, Afternoon, Night) and Day Type (Weekday vs. Weekend).

Hourly Sales Traffic: An area chart mapping total sales across different hours of the day to identify exact customer rush hours.

Product Performance: Column and horizontal bar charts detailing the total sales revenue and transaction volumes for various coffee types (e.g., Americano with Milk, Latte, Cappuccino, Espresso).

#💡Key Insights & Final Conclusion:

Based on the dashboard analysis, several key business insights were uncovered:

Peak Seasonality: February, March, and October are the strongest performing months, driving the highest sales volumes of the year.

Rush Hours: Sales peak consistently around 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM, and 4:00 PM, highlighting the most critical hours for customer traffic. This indicates a strong need for optimized staffing and inventory readiness during mid-morning and late-afternoon shifts.

Customer Preferences: Americano with Milk and Latte emerged as the absolute top-selling coffee types, reflecting a strong customer preference for milk-based espresso drinks.

Weekly Trends: The data shows that Weekdays account for the vast majority of sales (75%) compared to weekends (25%), suggesting the primary customer base consists of daily commuters and office workers.
